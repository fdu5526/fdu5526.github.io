Welcome!

I am currently a senior Computer Science major at Carnegie Mellon University. I chose CS because of my constant desire to create things, and the computer is one of the best tools for completing that desire.

I am also grabbing an art minor, because art taps into another aspect of the brain that is different from the one used by CS but at the same time, still fulfills my desires to create.

Because of those choices, my main interests fall into game development, human computer interaction, machine learning, animation, and interactive art.
