Mortis

Make Players Feel Bad

What Is It?

"Mortis" is a 2D sidescrolling puzzle platformer made in XNA. We created this game for CMU's Game Creation Society in fall 2013. It won Gold award for the semester.

What Is It About?

The player controls Death. The main game mechanic revolves around the ability to take life force from NPCs and give it to others, which is then used to solve puzzles to reach the next level.

We developed this concept through an approach similar to Thatgamecompany's; we started off by defining a emotion to evoke inside players (in this case, horribleness), and expand the gameplay from that.

What Did I Do?

I joined a team of 16 others at the beginning of the semester. I worked as the lead artist and managed a team of 8. I organized workload distribution, communicated with members to aggregate their opinions, kept the visual style on track, and worked with the programming and design team to integrate our assets. I learned how to manage a team and merge their works into a finished product.
