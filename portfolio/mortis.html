Mortis

What Is It?

"Mortis" is a 2D sidescrolling puzzle platformer made in XNA.

What Is It About?

The player controls Death. The main game mechanic revolves around the ability to take life force from NPCs and give it to others, which is then used to solve puzzles to reach the next level.

What Did I Do?

I joined a team of 16 other people from CMU's Game Creation Society (GCS) in September 2013. I worked as the art lead and managed a team of 8. I organized workload distribution, communicated with members to hear their opinions, kept the visual style on track, and worked with the programming and design team to integrate our assets. I learned how to manage a team and merge their works into a finished product. This game won GCS Gold at the end of the semester.
