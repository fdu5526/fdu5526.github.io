Long Live the King

   Smell-Based Strategic Drinking Game

What Is It?

So what does a game designer do the month he turns 21? He makes "Long Live the King", a drinking game about deceit, trust and betrayal, where the main mechanics of the game are accusing other players and smelling with your nose.

What Is It About?

The game is inspired by the fact that water and vodka look alike but smell very differently. From here, we constructed a world where a king who cannot smell is having a party with his subjects, half of whom are loyalists (L) and half of whom conspire to poison him (C), but no one knows who is who. At the start of each turn, each player is given 2 glasses, each can either be a shot of water or vodka. long live the king toast see rules at bottom for full description on how game works
