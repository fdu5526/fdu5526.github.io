Outfit Swap

What Is It?

"Outfit Swap" is a puzzle-adventure web game made in Javascript.

What Is It About?

The player starts off as a burger flipper dissatisfied with his job, and decides to rise through the social ladder by understanding the inner desires of other characters in the game to swap outfits with them.

What Did I Do?

I joined a team of 15 other people from CMU's Game Creation Society (GCS) in September 2012. I worked as an artist and communicator between the art and programming team. I learned about the difficulty of working with different types of people. This game won GCS Gold at the end of the semester.

Where To Play It?

The link to the game is down below. Just click on the link and the game will run in your web browser. We recommend using Mozilla Firefox or Google Chrome as they has the best performance running our game.
