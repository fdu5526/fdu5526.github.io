         Coyote

     A Board Game About Human Smugglers

What Is It?

Coyote is a board game themed around illegal human smuggling. It was made for Tracy Fullerton's game design class at USC IMGD.



The rules of the game.

What Is It About?

Each player is a Coyote tasked with smuggling Braceros across the border up into the Safehouse. On their way up, players must avoid the Border Patrol from catching them and try to steal from other players making the same journey. Due to resource shortage, at times players must leave Braceros behind, or they could push on forward while disregarding their Braceros' wellbeing. As each Bracero makes it to the Safehouse or dies on the journey, players tell stories about how this came to be. In the end, the player who guides the most Braceros to safety wins the game.



The initial setup of the game.

How Was It Made?

The game was made over the course of 4 weeks in a team of 3. It went through multiple iterations and was heavily playtested on. My role in the team was to create the mechanics, test it for balance, and conducting and observing playtests for each iteration.
