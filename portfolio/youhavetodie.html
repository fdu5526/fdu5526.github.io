You Have to Die

   A Karmic Adventure for Global Game Jam

What Is It?

We made this game for Global Game Jam 2015, which is a 48-hour hackathon for making games in teams. The theme of the Game Jam is "What do we do now?".



In this life, the player controls a door that attacks passerby

What Is It About?

The player takes on multiple lives through a journey of redemption. We made this to fit the theme, as each life has a different art style and control scheme (while still using the same buttons), thus we keep making the players ask "what do we do now?".



When the player becomes a potato, the world changes to a cut out style to signfy the changes that the player has to overcome

How Was It Made?

On a team of five, we made this game in Unity within the span of 48 hours. My main role was both a programmer and a producer for our team.



The player is now a farmer who has to farm to stay nourished

What Did I Learn?

I learned how to make a game starting from just a prompt to finished product within a tight and stressful time period. During those 48 hours, I had to combine everybody's brainstorming ideas, assign each member roles, and make sure they constantly have something to contribute while still programming my own work. I also learned to make calculated sacrifices, as some features are just too difficult to implement within what little time we had. Finally, I learned that the Global Game Jam is fun and I would definitely do it again.
