I'm Not Comfortable with This

Dating Sim with a Twist

What Is It?

"I'm Not Comfortable with This" is a web game made in Javascript which blends elements from both puzzle games and visual novels.



What Is It About?

Four computer science majors, realizing that the stereotype of CS majors being unsociable and fail at dating applies to themselves as well, decide to create a robot that would assist them during their dates so everything would go smoothly. The player assumes control of this robot to help the clueless CS majors in their quest for love.

What Did I Do?

I was a member of a 4 person team that started brainstorming in October 2012. I created the character designs and the level art and worked with the programming team in putting together the finishing touches in December 2012. I learned how to make a finished product from ground up with a short constraint of time.



What Is the Motive?

We wanted to make a silly game with a wacky story and at the same time poke fun at the CS major stereotypes that we all faced at some point in our college career.
