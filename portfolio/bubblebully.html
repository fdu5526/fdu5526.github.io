Bubble Bully

 Mess Up a Creature's Hard Work

What Is It?

In this game, a creature is frantically arranging bubbles into a grid, and the player can choose to use the mouse to mess with its efforts.



A video of the creature building its grid, then the player messes it up

What Are the Controls?

Click on any bubble to move it. If the creature is holding it, you will knock the bubble out of its grasp, and it will be frightened. If you violently shake the mouse in the bubble grid it built, it will disperse all the bubbles, making the creature go berzerk.



The creature attempt to create an orderly grid from the chaotic bubbles

What Is It Written In?

The game is written in Processing, the graphics are made in Adobe Photoshop, and it uses Box2D as its physics engine. It was written in 2 weeks.



A swipe of the mouse can easily mess up the work

What Is the Motive?

I made this game to experiment with Box2D, a physics engine used in many games. I then used it to implement an AI for players to interact with. I want the player to feel entertained, but at the same time, feel bad for messing with a powerless creature, and will hopefully end up feeling emotionally connected to it.
