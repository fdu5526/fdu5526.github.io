What Is It?

The Curious Tale of an Unexpected Visit is a 2D sidescrolling narrative game made in Unity. All of the visuals of its collage art style comes from internet archives. I worked in a team of 2 over the course of a semester for Richard Lemarchand's game production class. The game typically takes 5-10 minutes to play through.
