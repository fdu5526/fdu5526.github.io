Image Analogy

Generate Photographs from Low Budget Drawings

What Is It?

This computer program takes in a real photograph, a low budget drawing of it, and a new low budget drawing, and then generates a fourth image that shapes like the third image but has the texture of the actual photograph. This allows me to generate new photographs by using very simple drawings.


In all of the pictures, top left is actual photograph, top right is low budget drawing of it. Bottom left is a new low budget drawing, and bottom right is what my program generates.



What Is It Written In?

It is written in Matlab.
