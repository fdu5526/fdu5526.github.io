      Bearoness

Seduce the Prince to Lift the Curse of Being a Bear

What Is It?

"Bearoness" is an isometric stealth game where the player controls a princess cursed to become a bear. In order to lift the curse, she has to find true love by infiltrating a fancy castle ball and winning the prince's heart (while being a bear).



Knocking over people or running on 4 legs will raise suspicion

What Is The Main Mechanic?

The bear has 2 control schemes for movement; either she can sacrifice stealth for speed by running on 4 legs, or she can wobble on 2 legs to avoid suspicion, because when she wears gown and walks 2 legs she obviously look human. However, with that wobbleness, she might bump into people and objects, which also raise suspicion. When suspicion becomes too high, people realize she is a bear and flee, thereby not allowing her to reach the prince. However, she can find coat closets inside the castle, where she can change into a different dress, causing everyone to think there is once again no longer bears on the premise, allowing our heroine to continue her quest for love.



The player has to overcome challenges such as spiking the drink of a rival princess

How Was It Made?

The game is made in Unity over the course of 7 weeks by a team of 5 people. I worked as the team's producer and lead programmer. In my producer role, I assigned tasks to group members, kept our production schedule on track, and ensured positive group dynamic. As a programmer, I designed the software structure, implemented the bulk of the game mechanics, and imported assets into the engine.



For the final boss fight, the player has to seduce the prince via a dance

What Did I Learn?

This is my first 3D game, so I learned to integrate the pipeline between Unity and Maya. Next, being the main programmer made me pretty proficient at using Unity. Thirdly, I learned to manage a long-term production schedule for 5 people, which required me to make many adjustmentss to accomodate constant changes and emergencies, such as a group member breaking her leg during the final crunch week. Finally, I realized that I really had fun sitting around brainstorming crazy game ideas with my group.
